Mini Crosspaths
</h1>

Adds a number of mini two-upgrade Paths++ paths for towers. 


Many are based on old BTD game features / my favorite features that I've implemented in other mods such as Mega Knowledge or Rogue Remix, others are brand new.
This makes them available in a balanced way in regular games, making you pay a fair price and consider the crosspathing tradeoffs
(unless you have Paths++ Balanced Mode turned off).

## Splodey Dart Monkeys Path

<img src='SplodeyDartMonkeys/HeatTippedDarts.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Heat Tipped Darts' >

#### 1. Heat Tipped Darts ($125)

Heat tipped darts allow the Dart Monkey to pop Frozen and Lead Bloons.

<img src='SplodeyDartMonkeys/SplodeyDarts.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Splodey Darts' >

#### 2. Splodey Darts ($400)

<details>
<summary>Projectiles will create a small explosion the first time they collide with a Bloon.</summary>
Explosions have the same pierce as the projectile, and the same base damage (but unaffected by damage modifiers).
</details>

## Double Ranga Boomers Path

<img src='DoubleRangaBoomers/SonicBoom.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Sonic Boom' >

#### 1. Sonic Boom ($100)

<details>
<summary>Boomerangs can smash through Frozen Bloons for extra damage.</summary>
All projectiles can pop Frozen bloons, gaining a damage bonus equal to the projectile's base damage.
</details>

<img src='DoubleRangaBoomers/DoubleRanga.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Double Ranga' >

#### 2. Double Ranga ($400)

<details>
<summary>Throws 2 Boomerangs at a time, but not as frequently.</summary>
Main boomerangs, MOAB Press Boomerangs, and orbiting glaives have double projectile count but two thirds attack speed.
</details>

## Laser Rang Boomers Path

<img src='LaserRangBoomers/LaserRangs.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Laser Rangs' >

#### 1. Laser Rangs ($250)

<details>
<summary>Now throws Laser-Rangs, gaining increased pierce and projectile speed.</summary>
Projectiles can longer pop Purple Bloons, but travel 25% faster and have pierce increased by +4 or x1.5, whichever is higher, with MOAB press getting a flat +60.
</details>

<img src='LaserRangBoomers/PlasmaRangs.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Plasma Rangs' >

#### 2. Plasma Rangs ($350)

<details>
<summary>Upgrades to Plasma-Rangs, attacking even faster.</summary>
Can pop Frozen and Lead Bloons. Fire rate for all weapons is 50% faster, including Glaive-Lord orbital damage.
</details>

## Instant Bomb Shooters Path

<img src='InstantBombShooters/QuantumPayload.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Quantum Payload' >

#### 1. Quantum Payload ($150)

Bombs explode instantly at the target's location with no travel time.

<img src='InstantBombShooters/PrecisionMunition.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Precision Munition' >

#### 2. Precision Munition ($500)

<details>
<summary>Can hit Bloons it can see anywhere on screen. Attacks 30% slower for very far away Bloons.</summary>
Gains global effective range. If Bloons are further than twice the original displayed range, attacks 30% slower.
</details>

## Dreadnought Boats Path

<img src='DreadnoughtBoats/Dreadnought.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Dreadnought' >

#### 1. Dreadnought ($600)

<details>
<summary>Shoots molten cannonballs instead of darts/grapes that deal extra damage and can pop all Bloon types.</summary>
Projectiles can hit all Bloon types, and their base damage and additive bonuses are all increased by +1 or x1.3, whichever is higher.
</details>

<img src='DreadnoughtBoats/Privateer.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Privateer' >

#### 2. Privateer ($800)

<details>
<summary>Earns 50% more cash from Bloons popped / hooked. Merchantman income is increased by 10%.</summary>
Cash from Bloons is 1.5x, Merchantman income is +10% after other additive bonuses like Trade Empire.
</details>

## Hover Plane Aces Path

<img src='HoverPlaneAces/HoverPlane.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Hover Plane' >

#### 1. Hover Plane ($600)

<details>
<summary>Now longer flies in paths, instead hovering wherever you direct it.</summary>
Replaces all default movement with Heli Pilot style movement.
</details>

<img src='HoverPlaneAces/Pursuit.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Pursuit' >

#### 2. Pursuit ($500)

<details>
<summary>A new targeting option enables Monkey Ace to seek and pursue the Bloons automatically.</summary>
Gains the Pursuit targeting option like Heli Pilots.
</details>

## Crystal Ball Wizards Path

<img src='CrystalBallWizards/MonkeyApprentice.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Monkey Apprentice' >

#### 1. Monkey Apprentice ($75)

<details>
<summary>Further study makes all Wizard projectiles travel much further.</summary>
All projectiles that travel in a straight line have doubled lifespan.
</details>

<img src='CrystalBallWizards/CrystalBall.png' height=50 align='right' alt='Crystal Ball' >

#### 2. Crystal Ball ($1,000)

<details>
<summary>Gains a toggle to allow long range targeting of Bloons in radius of your other towers. Loses Guided Magic's obstacle ignoring behavior while active.</summary>
Toggle Advanced Intel style targeting at the cost of giving up Guided Magic ignoring blockers, if you have it. Projectiles will always use Guided Magic homing to hit targets.
</details>
